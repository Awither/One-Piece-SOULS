Panel 1 – Soul Rating & Soul Bank

Core Soul Rating

Input the creature’s overall strength, training, and willpower. The app calculates a Soul Score, Soul Level (SoL), and Soul Energy (SPU) up into the thousands. Willpower matters the most.

Physical and combat capability. 1 = weak, 10 = monstrous physical threat.

How trained / legendary they are in combat and technique.

Soul resolve, haki, mental fortitude. Willpower is the biggest factor in soul strength.

Soul Score ≈ Raw Might × 2 + Proficiency Tier × 3 + Willpower × 5.
SoL ≈ Soul Score / 10 (1–10).
Soul Energy ≈ Soul Score × 15 (peak beings: 1000+).
Max HP lost is a flavor suggestion for how much life force is ripped out.

Soul Bank

All harvested souls are stored here with their Soul Energy value. Mark which souls are available for crafting, and use them later to fuel homies, domains, or abilities.

Panel 2 – Homie Workshop

Create a Homie

Spend Soul Energy (SPU) once to create Minor, Territory, Buff, or Signature Homies. Each homie tracks its total SPU investment and can be upgraded or revived later.

For personality, flavor, or mechanical links. Purely for your reference.

This is the Soul Energy you’re dedicating to bring the homie into existence.

Homie Roster & Upgrades

Upgrade HP, AC, Damage, and Utility tiers by spending SPU. When a homie is destroyed, pay half of its total SPU investment to revive it.

Homie Attack Forge (AI)

Pick a homie, describe the attack, choose a power level and effect types, and the souls will forge a full D&D-style attack for you (high-tier moves can hit like 30d12+).

1 = tiny cantrip-style move, 10 = elaborate multi-step finisher with huge damage.

Panel 3 – Domains & Territories

Create a Domain

Build Big Mom–style territories. Invest SPU, set a passive fear DC, and bind Territory Homies to shape the land. The domain’s personality and stats can later drive AI-generated lair actions.

Hold Ctrl/Cmd to select multiple Territory Homies.

Domains Overview & Lair Actions

Manage your Domains: see their Fear pressure, control radius, and which Territory Homies guard them. Use AI to generate and reroll lair actions tailored to each domain.

Panel 4 – Ability Cards (Manual & AI)

Create Ability (Manual or AI)

Build abilities by hand, or let the souls handle the heavy lifting by setting power level, effect types, and desired outcomes. AI-generated abilities will appear in the Ability List.

Ability List

Browse all abilities – manual and AI-generated. Each card is editable and has a Copy button so you can paste it into character sheets or notes.

Quick Reference Setup

This panel is designed for printing. Add summary entries for players, allies, homies, or entire domains. Print mode hides all other panels for a clean table reference.
